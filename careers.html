CAREER

Plus Medical Rehabilitation Centre prioritizes job equality in recruitment processes, treating all individuals with fairness and respect. Recruitment practices focus on equal opportunity and non-discrimination, evaluating candidates based on qualifications and experience. The centre aims to build a diverse team, encouraging innovation and personal growth.

At Plus Medical Rehabilitation Centre, we maintain a transparent recruitment policy. While we are not actively hiring at the moment, any future job opportunities will be promptly announced and posted on our website. We encourage individuals interested in joining our team to regularly visit our website for updates and notifications regarding job openings. Your interest in a career with us is valued, and we look forward to connecting with potential candidates when positions become available.
