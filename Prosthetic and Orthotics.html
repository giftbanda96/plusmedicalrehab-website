Prosthetic and Orthotics

Our Prosthetic and Orthotic facility is more than just Prosthetic and Orthotics depertment. Besides being in the forefront of prosthetic technology and development around the globe, we’re here to give anyone who has lost a limb and body function the tools, information, and resources necessary to allow them to achieve their goals.

We provide healthcare, advocacy, and educational services so that you can live an optimal and high quality life

A prosthesis is a device that replaces a missing body part, in our case limbs. Conditions leading to loss of limbs (amputation) include :
1. Trauma - RTA, Gunshots, workplace accidents etc

2. Chronic Diseases - Diabetes, Cancer etc

3. Infections - osteomyelitis, non healing wounds

4. Congenital malformation - being born without a limb or a limb that is not fully developed

Orthosis is a device that can correct a deformity, accommodate/ support a limb, or compensate a missing function. Conditions that can be managed by orthosis include but not limited to:
1. Neurogical conditions - Stroke, CP, traumatic brain damage etc

2. Musculoskeletal - Fractures, unstable joints

3. Traumatic injuries- Sprains and strains

4. Conjenital - knee hyper-extension, Lower limb descrepancy/
