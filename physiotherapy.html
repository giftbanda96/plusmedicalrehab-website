PHYSIOTHERAPY

We provide physiotherapy services that aim at
promoting quality of life by developing, restoring and maintaining people’s
maximum movement and functional ability.

We manage a wide range of conditions in the various aspects of physiotherapy. Some of these conditions have been described below;

These are conditions that affect your bones, ligaments, muscles and joints. They can impede ones movement ability thereby leading to inability to independently do activities of daily living optimally. Most of these conditions can affect both children and adults

Such conditions include; Low and upper back pain, Disc herniation, Arthritis (Gout, Rheumatoid, Osteoarthritis and septic), fractures, neck pain, Joint pain, etc..

These are conditions that affect your brain, spinal cord and peripheral nerves. They can lead to weakness, spasticity, tremor, impaired cognition and coordination and inability to execute the desired movement in the best possible way.

Some of these conditions are stroke, traumatic and pathological spinal cord injuries, brain injuries, Parkinsonism, TB spine, foot drop, etc.

These conditions mostly affect the lungs, the heart and blood vessels. They limit the perfusion of tissues which can lead to damage and eventually death.

The following are some of the conditions under this category; Chronic obstructive disease (COPD), Empyema, Congestive Cardiac Failure (CCF), Restrictive Pulmonary Diseases etc.

These conditions mainly affect children. They limit normal growth and development of children and may lead to lifelong disability.

Examples of such conditions include; Cerebral palsy, Autisms, Hydrocephalus, Developments delay, Flat foot, spinal bifida, Arthrogryposis, club foot, erb’s palsy etc.

Injuries caused by exposure to heat, chemicals and radiation can lead to deformities and disabilities if not treated with physiotherapy. These conditions can lead to joint stiffness especially when it occurs along a joint.

We restore function, prevent contracture, and eliminate possible disabilities in clients that have sustained burns.

Women can have several impairments that are associated with pregnancy or their reproductive system, which can affect their optimal movement function. Therefore we have special programs that are aimed at promoting women’s health.

Conditions that can affect women are; symphisislysis, low back pain (During and after pregnancy), Incontinence, Paraplegia (post- delivery).
