ABOUT US

Plus Medical Rehabilitation Centre (PMRC) Limited is outpatient clinic that aims at facilitating optimal function of people with various disabilities, impairments and participation restrictions in order to become effective.

Our mission is to offer high standard, evidence-based and comprehensive programs that are aimed at restoring and optimizing mental and physical function of our clients, in order to promote their quality of life.

To help all clients across the globe have an optimal and high quality life through the administration of therapeutic programs that aim at eliminating impairments, activity limitations and participation restrictions thereby enabling them to actively participate in and contribute to their society.

• Empathy – We have compassionate healthcare professionals.

• Quality – We provide high standard, evidence based and whole-patient management.

• Integrity – We are authentic and trustworthy. We take full responsibility of our clients as we manage them. We are absolutely accountable and transparent in our management.

• Excellence – We value excellence in our management and in our conduct as individual professionals.

• Commitment – We are committed to make sure our clients’ and stakeholders’ needs are not only met but surpassed.

• Professionalism – We are greatly professional when we handle our clients. To ensure professional development we regularly participate in professional development activities so that we are well informed on the best evidence based treatment modalities in the recent research literature. This helps us to maintain a high standard of care for our clients.

• Confidentiality – We ensure that patients’ information is kept confidential and concealed even after they die.
